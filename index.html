About

Oliver is a 6-year-old Pembroke Welsh Corgi.

History

Oliver is a 6 year old Pembroke Welsh Corgi that was born on April 11, 2016. He is a tri-color corgi. He was born in Tulsa, OK and currently resides in Tulsa, OK.

Personality

Oliver is a bit standoffish to people he does not like and can be selective with who he chooses to bond with. He loves his mother and his other corgi sibling. He is very friendly and has a loud bark.

Hobbies

Oliver enjoys running and frapping in the back yard and chasing the neighbors dog. He loves food and to eat and knows many tricks. He also loves walks and belly scratches.
